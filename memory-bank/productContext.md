# Product Context

## Purpose
To showcase Ali Yılmaz's identity as a creative maker who blends acting, voiceover, and AI-assisted digital production into a single, cohesive personal brand, while maintaining clear boundaries between the two distinct professional fields.

## Problems Solved
- Moving beyond a traditional, one-dimensional "software developer" or "actor" profile to a "creative producer/persona".
- Providing a central, professional hub to showcase audio demos, video acting performances, and digital web projects side-by-side.
- **Inquiry Management:** Preventing mixed professional inquiries by explicitly routing software/AI requests to one email and media/acting requests to another.

## User Experience Goals
- **Instant Understanding:** Visitors should immediately grasp his unique multi-disciplinary nature through highly direct, non-poetic language.
- **Aesthetic Impact:** A sophisticated, highly refined, and minimalist dark interface. It should feel calm, clean, and personal (avoiding over-the-top agency or cinematic manifestos).
- **Exploration & Action:** Clear division between physical performances (Acting & Voiceover) and digital/AI productions (Projects), heavily guided by functional and straightforward CTAs. Contact options are distinctly categorized to reduce cognitive load.

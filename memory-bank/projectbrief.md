# Project Brief

## Overview
- **Project Name:** Ali Yılmaz Personal Portfolio & Brand Website
- **Owner:** Ali Yılmaz (Voice actor, actor, digital creator using AI tools / vibe coding)
- **Language:** Turkish
- **Goal:** Create a professional, clear, and highly personal portfolio that presents his physical and digital production talents side-by-side, while routing inquiries explicitly to their respective channels to prevent overlap.

## Core Vision
Ali Yılmaz operates at the intersection of acting, voiceover artistry, and AI-assisted digital production. The portfolio acts as a quiet, confident, straightforward platform to introduce him, list his performances, showcase his primary software projects (like Pengui), and offer distinct, separated ways to connect based on the professional need.

## Design Identity
- **Visual Direction:** A minimalist and pure dark interface relying heavily on neutral greys (`zinc`) and strict sans-serif readable typography (`Geist`). Absolutely no flashy "wow" elements—the content itself provides the value.
- **Tone:** Natural, simple, and direct. Moving strictly away from poetic, dramatic, or "agency-manifesto" language. The communication remains grounded, factual (e.g., student, born 2007), friendly, and deeply personal.
